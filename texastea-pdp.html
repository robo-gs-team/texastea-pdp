THE STORY

FROM THE SUGARCANE FIELDS OF SUGAR LAND, TEXAS.

Sugar Land grew its first sugarcane in 1838. The same pure cane sugar grown in those fields sweetens every bottle of this tea.

Not a flavoring. Not a syrup. The real thing — named for the place it comes from, traceable to the source.

Brewed with organic black tea, balanced with cold-pressed lemon juice, bottled in glass. That is the entire recipe.
